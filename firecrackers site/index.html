About Firecrackers Football Factory

We're a football club and talent accelerator developing young footballers in Nigeria.

Firecrackers FC is a dynamic and ambitious football accelerator established to reignite the spirit of sportsmanship, talent development, and community pride in Nigeria.

Built on a foundation of passion, determination, and the pursuit of excellence, Firecrackers FC is more than just a football team—it's a movement that inspires and empowers local talent to reach their full potential.

Our Vision

To become Africa’s premier talent bridge – transforming the continent’s top 1% of football potential into dominant global professionals

Our Mission

We rescue elite African talents from the ‘valley of the shadow of death’ through our 5-phase accelerator ensuring no exceptional player falls through the cracks.
